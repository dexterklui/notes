# Fixed-income

## Intro

Fixed-income is a broad class of financial products that comprises any
investment where the investor earns a set payment on a pre-determined schedule.

### Common Fixed-income Products

- Bonds
- Vanilla and exotic derivatives
- Asset-backed securities (ABS)
- Mortgage-backed securities (MBS)
- Preferred securities
